NU Lipa is committed to nation-building through education. NUCLICKS extends this mission beyond our campus walls, reaching out-of-school youth in our community.

What is NUCLICKS?

NUCLICKS (NU Community Learning Initiative for Computer Knowledge and Skills) is a learning management system developed by National University — Lipa. It is specifically designed for out-of-school youth and community members who want to learn about computers and improve their digital literacy.

  • Free & Accessible — No tuition fees; open to all community members.
  • NU Lipa Accredited — Courses designed and recognized by NU Lipa faculty.
  • Flexible Learning — Self-paced modules that fit your schedule.
  • Community Impact — Improving digital literacy one learner at a time.
